WHAT IS PROJECT IM

An art programme with buzz - Think funn art experiments and using creativity to find real solutions to real world problems. We see the worl through children's eyes because, honestly, we think it's pretty cool!

HOW DOES IT WORK

No boring lessions - we engage the senses because we lvoe children's curiousity for...well...everything! Our hands-on and fun activities culmunate in art projects and visual diaries. We teach kids to be master artists as well as master thinkers too!

WHY HERE, WHY NOW
