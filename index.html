WHAT IS PROJECT IM

WHAT IS PROJECT IM

An art programme with buzz - Think fun art experiments and using creativity to find real solutions to real world problems. We see the world through children's eyes because, honestly, we think it's pretty cool!

HOW DOES IT WORK

HOW DOES IT WORK

No boring lessons - we engage the senses because we love children's curiosity for...well...everything! Our hands-on and fun activities culminate in art projects and visual diaries. We teach kids to be master artists as well as master thinkers too!

WHY HERE, WHY NOW

WHY HERE, WHY NOW

We want to give art a voice to inspire creative thinking with a purpose. It's what we call design thinking, where ideas and solutions meet to make some real good impact. We believe that nurturing young minds to become imaginative go-getters will one day save our world!
